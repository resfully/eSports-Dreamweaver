Article - Winning With Communication: The Importance of Communication in Esports
1) How important is communication in esports? In other words, how does communication help with success in the gaming industry? There are many examples that can answer this question, however one of the most iconic comes from the 2013 League of Legends World Championships. In the finals match of the tournament, Korean-based team T1 won the series 3-0 against the Royal Club team. A T1 player had stated that they had not started off too well, but were able to make many comebacks despite this because of their excellent communication. On the other hand, the Royal Club was having many communication problems causing them to make poor decisions during the match. This shows that communication can make a huge difference between winning and losing. However, it can also be used to help team morale when players are starting to tilt. For instance, North American team Cloud 9 is famous for having very good morale due to their strong relays. Cloud 9 CEO and founder Jack Etinenne stated that they place a lot of emphasis on communication within their teams because he thinks it is one of the most important factors to a successful team. The examples of T1 and C9 are show that communicating with your teammates and coach is essential to win. To improve communication, you should be clear and concise, listen to your teammates, practice communicating in general, be patient, and be flexible.
